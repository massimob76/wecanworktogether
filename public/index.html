More about me:

I'm currently working as a freelance designer in London.

I specialize in digital design, even though I've been involved in a wide range of projects ranging from broadcast animation, web, IPTV to print design.

I've attained a design degree (Information design) in 2000 and have been involved in the design industry for nearly 10 years now.

I'm confident working independently or as part of a team.
